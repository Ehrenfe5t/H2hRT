# V11 Parametric Antenna Benchmarks

The `dipole`, `patch`, and `horn` CSV files are generated by
`generate_parametric_patterns.py`. They are controlled analytic benchmarks,
not HFSS or chamber-measurement data.

- Dipole gain uses the half-wave dipole closed-form field and is normalized to
  approximately 2.15 dBi peak and unit spherical-average gain.
- Patch gain uses a front-hemisphere `cos(theta)^2.75` model, 6 dBi peak,
  approximately 76 degree HPBW, and a -30 dBi back floor.
- Horn gain uses `cos(theta)^22`, 15 dBi peak, approximately 28 degree HPBW,
  and a -50 dBi back floor.
- Jones files are normalized Ludwig-3 co/cross components. Patch and horn use
  bounded, angle-dependent synthetic cross-polar leakage with complex phase so
  the polarimetric pipeline can be verified. They must not be presented as a
  measured model of a particular antenna.

Every generation checks that spherical-average gain does not exceed one. A
future HFSS or measured model can replace these files without changing the
experiment or analysis pipeline, provided its provenance and coordinate
convention are recorded.
